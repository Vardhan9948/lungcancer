About LungCare AI

🧠

How It Works

Our system analyzes multiple risk factors including demographics, smoking history, environmental exposures, medical history, and current symptoms using a weighted medical scoring algorithm based on epidemiological research.

🎯

Cancer Types Detected

  • Adenocarcinoma — Most common (~40%)
  • Squamous Cell — Smoking-related (~25%)
  • Small Cell (SCLC) — Aggressive (~15%)
  • Large Cell — Less common (~5-10%)
  • Benign — Non-cancerous conditions
📋

What You Get

  • Cancer type probability scores
  • Overall risk level assessment
  • Specific precautions to follow
  • Treatment options by stage
  • Medicine recommendations
